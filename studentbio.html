Hello! My name is Alexia. Born and raised in AZ but I have lived in southern Cali and parts of Mexico briefly. Growing up I enjoyed reading, riding bikes, dancing, playing softball, and all fun things. In school I had a passion for Arts, Math, and Design. Emphasize on my passion for Math, I really thought I was ment to be an Algebra Teacher but plans have changed. My life consists of being a mommy to my 3 babies :) Their names are Ace, Enzo, and Chevelle. I was very fortunate to have been a stay at home momma for 5yrs but at the end of 2019 I became a single mother and started working full time with my Esthetician license. With my Esthetics license I thought I was going to focus on my MUA(Makeup Artist) path. I was doing makeup applications for small events, facials, dermabrasions, and lash extensions. At-home services weren't working out for me as a Homemaker/Mother with no support, so I stopped. Right now I'm at European Wax Center and have made so much success that clients have been booking out months in ahead to save a spot. Having been an Esthetician for 5 years and my youngest ready to go to school, its now time to transition for something better. Networking with my clients has been a game changer and has led me here to this Bootcamp. Who wouldve thought?! LOL. Lets go Girls Who Code!
